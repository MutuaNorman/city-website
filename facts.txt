1. Dubai is the most populous city in the United Arab Emirates (UAE).
2. Dubai is known for its ultra-modern architecture, vibrant nightlife, and luxury shopping.
3. Dubai is also home to the world’s tallest building, the Burj Khalifa, standing at 830 meters tall.
4. Dubai has the world’s largest man-made harbor, Port Rashid.
5. Dubai is the financial hub of the Middle East, with the Dubai International Financial Centre being the largest financial center in the region.
6. Dubai is home to the world’s only seven-star hotel, the Burj Al Arab.
7. The Dubai Mall is the largest shopping mall in the world, with over 1,200 stores.
8. Dubai is home to some of the most luxurious hotels in the world, including the Atlantis The Palm, the Burj Al Arab, and the Jumeirah Emirates Towers.
9. Dubai is home to the world’s largest indoor ski resort, Ski Dubai.
10. In 2013, Dubai was named the “Happiest City in the Middle East” by the United Nations.
11. Its population is 3.331 million people
Tourist attractions include
1. Burj Khalifa: The world's tallest building stands at 830 meters and is a major tourist attraction in Dubai.

2. Dubai Mall: One of the world's largest shopping and entertainment destinations, Dubai Mall offers an array of attractions and experiences.

3. Palm Jumeirah: The iconic man-made island shaped like a palm tree is home to luxury resorts, shopping malls, and attractions.

4. Dubai Fountain: The world's largest choreographed fountain show is set to music and is situated in the Burj Khalifa Lake.

5. Dubai Creek: This saltwater estuary is the traditional heart of Dubai and offers a unique glimpse into the city's heritage.

6. Ski Dubai: The world's largest indoor ski slope is located in the Mall of the Emirates.

7. Burj Al Arab: This iconic sail-shaped hotel is one of the most recognizable landmarks in Dubai.

8. Desert Safari: Take a thrilling dune-bashing ride through the desert and enjoy a traditional Bedouin dinner under the stars.
